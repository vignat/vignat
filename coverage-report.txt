To replicate Table 1 from the paper, install `sloccount` and run the following commands from the nf/lib folder. (Note that RTE_SDK and RTE_TARGET are required by DPDK; mine are `export RTE_TARGET=x86_64-native-linuxapp-gcc; export RTE_SDK=/home/solal/dpdk`)

DPDK:
sloccount $RTE_SDK/lib/librte_eal/common/*.c $RTE_SDK/lib/librte_eal/linuxapp/eal/*.c $RTE_SDK/lib/!(librte_acl|librte_distributor|librte_power)/*.c $RTE_SDK/drivers/bus/pci/*.c $RTE_SDK/drivers/bus/pci/linux/*.c $RT
E_SDK/$RTE_TARGET/include/*.h $RTE_SDK/lib/librte_eal/common/*.h $RTE_SDK/drivers/bus/vdev/*.h $RTE_SDK/drivers/bus/pci/*.h

NIC driver:
sloccount $RTE_SDK/drivers/net/ixgbe/ixgbe_{fdir,flow,ethdev,ipsec,pf,rxtx,tm}.{c,h} $RTE_SDK/drivers/net/ixgbe/base/ixgbe_{api,common,phy,82599}.{c,h}

libVig, full - not used in paper but just FYI:
sloccount containers/* expirator.*

libVig, only NAT-related, used in the paper:
sloccount expirator.* containers/double-map.* containers/double-chain.* containers/double-chain-impl.* containers/map-impl.*

VigNAT:
sloccount flow.c flow.h flow-log.c flowmanager.c flowmanager.h flowtable.c flowtable.h nat_config.h nat_config.c nf_forward.h nf_log.h nf_time.c nf_time.h nf_util.c nf_util.h ../vignat/nat_forward_vignat.c ../nf_main.c
